### Day 3 — Wednesday June 10
**Post:** The 3 affiliate models — CPA vs RevShare vs Hybrid
**Format:** Carousel (5 slides)
**Pillar:** How It Works
**Platforms:** Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest
**Caption:**
There are 3 ways companies pay affiliates. Most people only know one.

CPA — Cost Per Action. You get paid a flat fee when someone completes an action (signup, purchase). Predictable but capped.

RevShare — Revenue Share. You earn a percentage of what the customer spends. Lower upfront, but compounds if they stay.

Hybrid — Both. Usually a smaller CPA + ongoing RevShare. Best of both worlds — if the terms are real.

The model you choose shapes your entire strategy. CPA rewards volume. RevShare rewards retention. Hybrid rewards patience.

Know what you're signing before you promote.

### Day 5 — Friday June 12
**Post:** How I evaluate every partnership offer in under 5 minutes
**Format:** Carousel (7 slides)
**Pillar:** Smart Moves
**Platforms:** Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest
**Caption:**
Before I say yes to any partnership, I run it through this 5-minute checklist.

Slide 1: What's the actual commission structure? (Not "competitive" — the number.)
Slide 2: What's the cookie duration? Under 7 days = walk away.
Slide 3: Do they have a dedicated partner manager? No contact = no support.
Slide 4: What's their product retention rate? High churn = high refund clawbacks.
Slide 5: Are the terms exclusive? If yes, what's the premium?

If a program can't answer these clearly, they're not ready for serious partners.
